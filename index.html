Bumper is a three-piece alternative indie band based in Washington, made up of Jayden Tierney (guitar, vocals), Amos Horne (bass, background vocals), and Maile Salois (drums, keys).

Drawing inspiration from artists like Jeff Buckley, Nirvana, Alice in Chains, and more, Bumper blends heartfelt melodies with raw, grunge-tinged energy.

Jayden and Amos first played together in another band, which eventually fell apart. After a break from performing, the two began jamming again and quickly realized they needed a drummer to bring their sound to life. Luckily, Amos had been dating Maile for a couple of years—and when he asked if she’d like to join, she said yes.

The band then proceeded to write and release their first single White Rose, and is currently working on their next album.
